CLIMB Website and Online Portal

Reimagining the study's website and participant experience of the entire mobile suite.
UI, UX, Web

Project Intro

CLIMB is a research study at UCSF headed by Dr. Bruno Biagianti that uses mobile technology to offer treatment and support for individuals experiencing or have experienced psychosis. The treatment includes cognitive training tasks as well as counselor and group therapy sessions via video calls.
As web manager and designer for the study, I reimagined what the interface and experience would be like if all aspects of the treatment were available on one online portal.

Landing page.

Log in page.

Dashboard and landing page for current participants.

I decided to implement a fixed sidebar where current tasks are displayed for easy continuation and placemarking. Reminders are also shown in the form of cards which can be easily scanned for all the essential information.
Progress on tasks is shown via a bar for easy visualization.
Minimized chat boxes are shown fixed at the bottom of the user's screen, much like Facebook's chat boxes, for easy accessibility.
