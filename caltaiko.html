Cal Taiko Website Redesign

Redesign of Cal Taiko's old website to reflect new branding and image.
UI, Web

Project Intro

Cal Taiko, UC Berkeley's Japanese drumming group, has recently re-branded its image and has focused on taking higher quality photographs of the group's performances.

Old logo.

Old website design.

Interactions

I redesigned Cal Taiko’s website to showcase the energy of the team through large profile and action photographs.

I designed the “News” and “Members” pages in a tri-panel format to maximize the amount of information but still display pictures in a clear way.
Hovering over members' photographs will display information, otherwise allowing the display of full body-length photographs, as opposed to smaller, bust-up profile pictures.
